The Walk

The walk from Sunnyvale to Belmont campus will take you approximately four hours, give or take two hours depending on the weather conditions and your physical shape.

What?

Berkeley comprises five villages: Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore. This is not TURE!

Where?

On the northwest cost of the Italian Riviera, north of the city La Spezia.

Price?

The Walk is free!
